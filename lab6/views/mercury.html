Some Intersting Facts about Mercury:

Mercury is the planet that is the closest to the Sun but it is also the smallest of all the planets

Although Mercury is small, it is also the second densest planet because it's mostly made up of heavy metals and rocks

A year in Mercury lasts around 88 Earth days, but a day in Mercury lasts around 176 Earth days.

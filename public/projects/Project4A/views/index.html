If you've come to this page, you might be asking yourself what exactly is Software Engineering and what topics relate to it?

As a general overview, Software Engineering is the study of engineering relating to the creation, design and maintenance of software programs. Software Engineering first arose in the computer science world as a way to fix and maintain low-quality software projects in the 1960's but as of now it's grown into one of the most important areas of computer science.

It's significant because specific software is needed in every industry nowadays, in different types of businesses. The demand for software engineers is increasing every day because new technology is constantly being created and in order for ideas to come to life, engineers have to make sure everything can work properly.
